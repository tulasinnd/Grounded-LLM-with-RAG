ApexNova Technologies - Product Catalog

ApexNova Technologies develops cloud-based software products for businesses.

Product 1: NovaDocs
NovaDocs is a document management platform that allows companies to organize, search, and securely store business documents. It supports version history, permission management, and AI-powered document search.

Product 2: NovaAssist
NovaAssist is an AI chatbot platform designed for customer support teams. It can answer frequently asked questions, summarize conversations, and generate support ticket responses.

Product 3: NovaAnalytics
NovaAnalytics is a business intelligence dashboard that provides sales reports, customer insights, and predictive analytics using machine learning models.

Product 4: NovaSecure
NovaSecure is a cybersecurity monitoring platform that detects unusual login activity, monitors network events, and generates security alerts in real time.

Product 5: NovaFlow
NovaFlow is a workflow automation platform that allows businesses to automate repetitive tasks using drag-and-drop workflows.

The most popular product is NovaAssist, with over 1,500 business customers worldwide.

The latest product released by the company is NovaSecure, launched in February 2025.

All products support cloud deployment and role-based access control. Customers receive 24/7 technical support through email, live chat, and phone.

The company's long-term goal is to integrate all products using a unified AI platform called NovaMind Enterprise.
