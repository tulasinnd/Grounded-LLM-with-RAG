ApexNova Technologies - Employee Handbook

Welcome to ApexNova Technologies. We are a software company founded in 2019 with headquarters in Bengaluru, India. Our mission is to build AI-powered software that improves productivity for businesses around the world.

The company currently has over 250 employees working across Engineering, Data Science, Product Management, Design, Human Resources, Sales, and Customer Success.

Employees follow a hybrid work policy. They are expected to work from the office on Tuesdays and Thursdays, while the remaining days can be remote.

Working hours are from 9:30 AM to 6:30 PM with a one-hour lunch break. Flexible working hours are available for employees with manager approval.

Each employee receives 24 days of paid annual leave, 12 days of sick leave, and 10 public holidays every year.

The Engineering department follows Agile development practices with two-week sprints. Every Friday from 3 PM to 5 PM is reserved for learning sessions where employees share technical knowledge.

The company's internal AI assistant is called NovaMind. Employees use NovaMind to search company documentation, summarize meeting notes, and answer technical questions.

Every new employee is assigned a mentor for the first three months. Quarterly performance reviews are conducted in March, June, September, and December.

The company cafeteria provides breakfast, lunch, and evening snacks free of charge. Employees also receive a yearly learning budget of ₹40,000 for books, online courses, certifications, and conferences.

ApexNova Technologies encourages continuous learning, collaboration, and innovation. Employees are expected to maintain high ethical standards and protect confidential company information.
